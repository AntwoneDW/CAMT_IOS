Blower
The indoor fan blower operates simultaneously with the
first heating element when the call for heat is given, as
well as after the elements are off. The fan operates at high
speed during the cooling mode and at low speed during
the heating mode.

Circulators
Move water through the hydronic system. They are made
up of a motor, a linkage, and an impeller, operating on
centrifugal force. The impeller slaps against the water and
throws it toward the outside of the housing where it is
forced from the pump at a higher pressure. The pressure
increases and pushes water around the piping circuit until it
eventually returns.
