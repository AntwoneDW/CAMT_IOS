Circuit Breaker and Fuses
Safety devices used to protect the
electrical system from short circuits
and overloads.
Circuit Breaker and Fuses
Circuit
A continuous loop of electrical current flowing along wires
or cables.
Conductor
Any material that allows electrical current to flow through it.
Copper wire is an especially good conductor.
Continuity
An uninterrupted electrical pathway through a circuit or a
fixture. A continuity test establishes if current could flow
through a circuit or fixture as it should.
Current
The movement of electrons along a conductor, such as
a wire.
