Safety: Your Key Responsibility
Electricity can be a dangerous thing. That’s why you must always follow the safety procedures you’ll be learning in this course,
as well as any that your company may require.
No matter how busy you may be, you can never take shortcuts. Your personal safety depends on it. Your residents’ safety
depends on it. And keeping the building in good condition depends on it, too.
