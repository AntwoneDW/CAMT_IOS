Heating Systems Safety
Here are the key safety rules to follow when working with heating systems.
Safety Rule #1: If Working with
Electricity, Turn the Power Off
If you’re repairing an electric heating system, always turn off
the power to that area and the electrical appliance itself.
( Note – Power should be shut off for both gas and electric.)
Then use a circuit tester (or multimeter) to make sure the
power is off . Never take chances. Verify everything.
