Cable
A cable is a group of two or more wires enclosed in a metal, rubber, or plastic
sheath.
  • Romex cable: Used in most apartment wiring.
  • BX cable: A flexible armored cable used in older buildings.
    • How to Read Non-metallic Cable
    Non-metallic cable is labeled with the number of insulated wires it contains, not
    including the grounding wire. For example: A cable printed with “12/2 G” contains
    two insulated 12-gauge wires, plus a bare grounding wire.
