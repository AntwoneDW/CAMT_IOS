Repairing a Leaking Cartridge Faucet
If you have a leaking cartridge faucet, replace the cartridge.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Be careful using a utility knife.
  • Replacement cartridge
  • O-rings
  • Heatproof grease
  • Screwdriver
  • Tongue and groove pliers
  • Utility knife
    • How-to Steps
    1. Shut off water to the faucet.
    2. Remove the faucet cap with a screwdriver.
    3. Using a screwdriver, unscrew the screw that holds the handle in place.
    4. Remove the handle.
    5. Unscrew (counterclockwise) the retaining ring with a tongue and groove pliers.
    6. Pull out the cartridge.
    7. Install a new cartridge with the tab in front.
    8. Twist off the spout.
    9. Cover the new O-rings with grease.
    10. Remove the old O-rings and replace them with new greased O-rings.
    11. Replace the spout.
    12. Replace the retaining ring.
    13. Replace the handle and handle screw.
    14. Replace the faucet cap.
    15. Turn on the water and check for leaks.
