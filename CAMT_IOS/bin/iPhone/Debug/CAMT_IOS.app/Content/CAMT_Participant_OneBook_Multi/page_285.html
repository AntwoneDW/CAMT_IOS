Adjust an Adjustable Primary Air Burner Shutter
When there is not enough air reaching the heat exchanger, one result is incomplete
combustion; another is reduced heat output. Try adjusting the burner air shutter.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Wear eye protection.
  • Screwdriver
    • Wear gloves.
    How-to Steps
    1. Set the thermostat to its highest setting to keep the burner running.
    2. Once the furnace has heated up, remove the burner access panel and loosen the locking screw.
    3. Open the shutter by turning it to the right until the blue base of the flame appears to lift slightly from the burner
    port surface.
    4. Close the shutter until the flame reseats itself on the surface.
    5. Replace the furnace panel.
