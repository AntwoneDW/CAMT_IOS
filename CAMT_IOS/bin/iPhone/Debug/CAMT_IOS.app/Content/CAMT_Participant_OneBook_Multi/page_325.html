Saturation Temperature
The second key concept about refrigerants is saturation
temperature, also known as the boiling point. It occurs when
the air conditioning system has equal parts of liquid and
vapor refrigerant. At saturation, the system can move the
heat from inside the building to outside the building.
Like the pressure-temperature relationship, knowing the
saturation temperature is critical to keeping a unit properly
charged.
