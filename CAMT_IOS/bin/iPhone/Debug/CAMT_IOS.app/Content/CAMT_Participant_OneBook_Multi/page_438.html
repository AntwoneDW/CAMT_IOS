Relighting a Pilot Light
Pilot lights occasionally go out. Here’s how to relight one.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Wear eye protection.
  • Match
    • How-to Steps
    1. Turn cooktop controls to OFF.
    2. Prop cooktop open.
    3. Light match and place near pilot light opening (between
    the two burners).
    4. Lower cooktop.
    5. Turn on burner and check operation.
