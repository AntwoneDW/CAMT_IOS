Working with Wires (Continued)
How to Strip Wires
1. Make sure power to the circuit is OFF.
2. Lock and tag out the circuit.
3. Use a combination tool to strip off about 1/4-inch
of insulation.
How to Connect Wires to Screw
Terminals
1. Use a combination tool to strip off about 1/4-inch of
insulation.
2. Twist wire end to form a half-circle.
3. Hook wire around screw terminal. The wire should curve
in a clockwise direction around the screw terminal.
4. Tighten screw terminal.
Note: There is better connection at screw terminal,
not push terminal hole.
