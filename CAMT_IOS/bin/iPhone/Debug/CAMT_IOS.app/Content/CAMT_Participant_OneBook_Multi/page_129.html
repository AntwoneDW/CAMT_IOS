Replacing a Ballast (Continued)
How-to Steps:
1. Turn off power to the circuit.
2. Remove the shade/diffuser.
3. Remove fluorescent tubes. (Twist the tubes until they unseat from the sockets.)
4. Remove the ballast cover.
5. Use a circuit tester or multimeter to see if the power has been turned off.
If You Use…
Then Follow These Steps…
A circuit tester
1. Touch one probe of the tester to the green grounding screw.
2. Insert the other probe into each wire connector.
3. Make sure the tester does not light. If it does, there is still power entering
the box.
A multimeter
1. Set the meter to AC volts.
2. Touch one probe of the meter to the green grounding screw.
3. Insert the other probe into each wire connector.
4. Make sure the meter shows 0 to 2 volts. If the number is higher, there is still
power entering the box.
6. Cut the wires to the ballast and unscrew the old ballast.
7. Install a new ballast with the same electrical ratings.
8. Attach the ballast wires to the socket wires, matching same color wires, connectors, screw terminal connections, or
push-in fittings.
9. Attach cover.
10. Put in tubes and twist until they are seated in sockets at both ends.
11. Put the diffuser back on.
12. Restore power to circuit and turn on light to make sure it works.
