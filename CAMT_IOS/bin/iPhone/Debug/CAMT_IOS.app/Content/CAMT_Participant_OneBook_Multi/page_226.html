Electric Water Heaters: Replacing the Heating Element
The most common problem with an electric water heater is a burned-out heating element.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Make sure the power is turned off.
  • Replacement heating element
    • Lock and tagout the main service panel.
  • Replacement gasket
  • Lockout/tagout device
    • Wear hand and eye protection.
  • Pipe joint compound
  • Screwdriver
  • Circuit tester or multimeter
  • Tongue and groove pliers
