Electrical Safety (Continued)
Safety Rule #2: Follow Lockout/Tagout Procedures
Where to Place Lockout Devices and Tags
Lockout devices and tags are typically applied to electrical circuit breakers or fuses, switches, and disconnects.
How to Lock and Tag Out
Your employer is required to train you on the complete
lockout and tagout process. But here’s a high-level look at
how it’s done:
1. Shut off the source of electrical power.
2. Attach a lockout device to this source, then lock and
tag it.
3. Release or drain any energy left in the electrical circuits
or equipment.
4. Test to be sure the energy is turned off and removed
from the circuits or equipment.
Once these lockout//tagout steps are complete, you can go
ahead with the electrical repair.
