Repairing a Disc Faucet
If you have a leaking disc faucet, clean or replace the ceramic disc cartridge.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Be careful when using a screwdriver to pry off things.
  • Replacement cylinder (if needed)
  • Screwdriver
  • Abrasive pad
    • How-to Steps
    1. Shut off the water to the faucet.
    2. Unscrew the screw that holds the handle in place.
    3. Remove the handle.
    4. Remove the top of the faucet.
    5. Unscrew (counterclockwise) the screws from the ceramic disc cartridge.
    6. Take out the ceramic disc cartridge.
    7. Pry out the seals in the ceramic disc cartridge.
    8. Clean the ceramic disc cartridge and replace seals. Or, install a new ceramic disc cartridge if it is worn.
    9. Replace the cartridge, top of faucet, and handle.
    10. Turn on the water and check for leaks.
