    How-to Steps: Installing the New Motor
    1. Install the new motor on the motor bracket.
    2. Install the motor shaft into the blower assembly.
    3. Secure the blower assembly to the motor housing.
    4. Center the squirrel cage in the housing and then secure the blower assembly to the motor shaft.
    5. Install the blower assembly into the furnace.
    6. Make the proper wiring connections.
    7. Turn the motor on and check it for the proper rotation.
    8. Inspect the rubber grommets that insulate the motor from the metal housing.
