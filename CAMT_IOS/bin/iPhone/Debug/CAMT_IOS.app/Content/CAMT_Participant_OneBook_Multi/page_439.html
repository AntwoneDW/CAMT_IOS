Adjusting the Height of a Pilot Light
Pilot lights that go out may need a height adjustment.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Wear eye protection.
  • Screwdriver
    • How-to Steps
    1. Turn cooktop controls to OFF.
    2. Prop cooktop open.
    3. Turn pilot adjustment screw counterclockwise to increase size of pilot light. (Screw may be on side of pilot, on pilot gas
    line, or behind burner control knob.)
    4. Adjust flame to 1/4 to 3/8 inches high.
    5. Lower cooktop.
    6. Turn on burner and check operation.
