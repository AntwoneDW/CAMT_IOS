Electrical Circuits
An electrical circuit is a connection of wires, switches, and receptacles that carry power
from the main service panel throughout an apartment and back to the service panel
again. Get ready to study diagrams!
Diagram of a Circuit
Each apartment will have several circuits—perhaps one for each room, and quite often, separate circuits for certain large
appliances, such as an electric range or an air conditioner.
A number of switches, receptacles, light fixtures, or appliances may be connected to a single circuit.
