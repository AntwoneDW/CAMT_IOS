Introduction
It’s now time to move away from the little stuff, and talk about the heart of any air conditioning unit—-that is, its refrigeration
system. In the next few hours, you’ll learn how to work with this system in a way that’s:
  • Safe
  • Fully compliant with the law
  • And will keep an air conditioner running at peak efficiency

    complex issues.
