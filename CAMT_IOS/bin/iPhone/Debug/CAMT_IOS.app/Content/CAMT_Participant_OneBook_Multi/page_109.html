Testing Receptacles (45 minutes)
There are many ways you can test a receptacle. Let’s look at them.
How to Use a Plug-in Tester
To test a receptacle with a plug-in tester:
1. Plug the tester into the receptacle.
2. Check the lights on the tester and use the reference to
identify the problem.
How to Use a Neon Circuit Tester to
Test for Power
To see if there’s power to a receptacle without removing the
cover plate:
1. Insert a tester probe into each slot of the receptacle.
2. If the bulb glows, there is still power to the receptacle;
turn off power to the circuit.
