Common Problems and Solutions
Where were you when the lights went out? Chances are you were somewhere that
needed lighting! In most cases, all that’s needed is to fix an incandescent light fixture is to
replace a burned-out bulb. But sometimes it takes a little more work. Here’s what you can
do about it.
