Introduction
So far, you’ve not only learned how air conditioning works, but you’ve also learned how to test and repair key parts of the
refrigeration, electrical, and air distribution systems.
We’re now going to look at all this information in a different context, that is, in terms of air conditioning problems and
solutions. What we’ll be covering is not an exhaustive list of everything that can go wrong with an air conditioning system, but
you can use this problems and solutions grid to help you get started down the correct troubleshooting path.
