How to Reset a Tripped Circuit Breaker
1. Open the service panel and find the “tripped” switch.
2. Flip the switch all the way to the OFF position.
3. Flip the switch to the ON position.
To test AFCI and GFCI circuit breakers, press the “Push to Test” button. The breaker should go to OFF. If not, the breaker will
need to be replaced. If a worn circuit breaker needs to be replaced, call an electrician.
How to Replace a Blown Plug Fuse
1. Open the service panel and find the blown fuse—you can see the melted metal through the window.
2. Unscrew the fuse by the rim.
3. Replace with a fuse that has the same amperage rating.
How to Test and Replace a Cartridge Fuse
1. Open the service panel and pull out the fuse block.
2. Use a fuse puller to remove cartridge fuse from fuse block.
3. Test fuse with continuity tester. (Fuse is good if light goes on.)
4. Return or replace fuse in fuse block. (Make sure fuse has same amperage rating.)
