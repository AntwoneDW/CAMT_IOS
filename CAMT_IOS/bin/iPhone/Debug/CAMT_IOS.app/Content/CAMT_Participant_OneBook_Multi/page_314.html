Air Conditioning Safety (continued)
Safety Rule #2: Wear Personal
Protective Equipment (PPE)
When working with air conditioners, wear the proper
personal protective equipment to protect yourself from
sparks, chemicals, cuts and scrapes, and burns.
This equipment includes:
  • Gloves
  • Eye shields or goggles
    • In addition, wear appropriate footwear. Make sure you have
    a sturdy shoe with a protective toe box and a non-slip sole.
