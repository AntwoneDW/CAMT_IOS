Galvanized Steel Pipe
Commonly used in water supply systems in older apartment
buildings. Galvanized steel pipes have a thin layer of zinc to
reduce corrosion.
Lock-Out/Tag-Out
A safety procedure to make sure that power and energy
sources are shut off properly and secured. Follow this
procedure when doing plumbing maintenance and repairs,
particularly when shutting off a water supply line or working
on an electrical appliance, such as a water heater.
