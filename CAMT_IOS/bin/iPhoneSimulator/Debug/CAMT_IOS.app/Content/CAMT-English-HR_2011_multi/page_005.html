Make-Ready Board
A tool that maintenance technicians and office personnel
use to track different tasks required to restore an apartment
back to its original condition after a resident moves out.
Make-Ready Checklists
A list of items in the apartment which must be inspected
and approved prior to a new resident move-in date.
Make-Ready Eye
Learn how to see a just-vacated apartment through
the eyes of the future resident, as well as those of your
management team or owner.
Make-Ready Maintenance
Also known as the Make-Ready Process. After a resident
moves out, the maintenance technician inspects the
apartment in order to assess the work that needs to be
done prior to the new resident’s move-in date.
