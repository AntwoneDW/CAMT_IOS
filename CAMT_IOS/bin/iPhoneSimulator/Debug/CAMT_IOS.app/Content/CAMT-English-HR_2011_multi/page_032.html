Air Conditioning
Compressor
Specialized pump that pulls cool,
low-pressure gas vapor refrigerant
from the evaporator through
a pipe called a suction line.
The refrigerant is compressed,
increasing its pressure and
temperature, and then pushed
through a pipe called the
discharge line.
Compressor
Compressor Motor Start Relay
Magnetically operated device consisting of a coil and
one or more sets of contacts that are used to connect or
disconnect the supply voltage circuit to the start winding of
a compressor.
Condensing Coils
With the help of a fan, these coils
condense the hot, high-pressure
vapor from the compressor into
a cooler, high-pressure liquid.
The coils have metal fins to help
dissipate heat.
