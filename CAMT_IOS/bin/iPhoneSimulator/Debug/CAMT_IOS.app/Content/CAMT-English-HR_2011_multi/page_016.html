Personal Protective
Equipment (PPE)
Equipment worn to minimize
exposure to a variety of
hazards. Examples of PPE
include such items as gloves,
foot and eye protection,
protective hearing devices
(earplugs, earmuffs) hard hats,
respirators and full body suits.
