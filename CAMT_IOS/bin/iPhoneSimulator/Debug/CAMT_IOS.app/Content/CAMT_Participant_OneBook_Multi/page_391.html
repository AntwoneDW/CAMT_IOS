Diagnosing the Root Cause of Appliance Problems
You get the call or read the service request – now what? There’s water pouring on the
floor from the dishwasher, a burner that doesn’t get hot, or a host of other issues.
Consider the steps you will take when diagnosing the cause of the problem and how you
will approach fixing it. Also take the order of the steps into consideration. What will you do
first, second, third, and so on? Use the words below as hints.
