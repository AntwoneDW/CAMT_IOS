Fixing a Stuck Garbage Disposal Flywheel
Food and other objects can get stuck in a garbage disposal and cause the flywheel to
stick. This can produce a humming sound, because the blades will not turn. It can also
shut off the power to the disposal.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Make sure the power to the disposal is off.
  • Lockout-tagout device
    • Lock and tagout the main service panel.
  • Hex (six-sided) offset wrench that
    • matches disposal (if equipped)
    Do not put your hands down the drain while working with the disposal.
    Consider wearing eye and hand protection.
