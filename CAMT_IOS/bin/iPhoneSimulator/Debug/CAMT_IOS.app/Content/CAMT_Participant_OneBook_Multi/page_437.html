Common Gas Cooktop Problems and Solutions
Because there are no moving parts to speak of, a gas cooktop is nearly trouble free.
Problem
Solution(s)
Gas cooktop: Pilot light is out or needs adjustment
1. The pilot light is out so you will need to relight it. See
page 50.
2. Pilot light height needs adjustment. Page 51 contains
instructions.
Gas cooktop: Burner will not light
1. The burner ports may be clogged. Clear them with a
paper clip, pin, or sewing needle.
2. The igniter may be faulty. To replace the igniter, see the
directions on page 52.
