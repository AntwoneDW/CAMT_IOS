    • 10.What is a GFCI Receptacle?
  • It is a receptacle equipped with a Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter safety
    • device. The GFCI constantly monitors electricity flowing in and out of a
    circuit, and can sense any loss of current. If the current flowing through
    the circuit differs by a small amount, the GFCI quickly switches off power
    to that circuit.
