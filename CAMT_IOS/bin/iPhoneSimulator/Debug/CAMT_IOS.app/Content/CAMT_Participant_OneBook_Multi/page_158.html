How to Fix Condensation on Pipes
Water condenses when cold and warm air meet. This often happens in a plumbing system when cold water passes through
pipes in a warm area of the building.
If condensation is a problem, it is best to insulate the water supply pipes with neoprene pipe insulation. Another option is to
wrap the pipe with fiberglass insulation and secure it with waterproof tape.
Keep in mind that it is hard to identify leaks in insulated water pipes.
