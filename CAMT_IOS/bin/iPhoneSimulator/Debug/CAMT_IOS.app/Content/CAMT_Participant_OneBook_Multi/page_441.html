Common Electric Oven Problems and Solutions
Ovens haven’t changed much over the years, except that the controls are more
sophisticated. You now can set controls to start and stop cooking at various times,
even check and regulate temperatures, so that dinner is ready when you are--if it works
correctly. If not, here’s how you can fix it.
Problem
Solution(s)
Electric oven: Inconsistent temperature
1. The temperature control may be faulty. To test and
replace the temperature control, see page 55.
2. The oven thermostat may need to be recalibrated; see
page 54.
Electric oven: Takes too long to heat the oven
The oven elements are broken or weak. Test and replace
the oven heating elements. Directions are on page 56.
