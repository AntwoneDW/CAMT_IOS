Common Electric Furnace Problems and Solutions
Most of the time, residents will report furnace problems with five words: “It’s too cold in
here!” Consider these possible causes as you troubleshoot.
Problem
Solution
Operational issues
1. Be sure the thermostat in the apartment is set to “heat” and is turned up to an
appropriate temperature.
2. Make sure the power switch on or near the furnace is turned on.
Airflow issues
1. See the information in the “Air Distribution” section.
Electrical issues
1. Check the service panel for a tripped breaker or blown fuse. Reset or replace
as necessary.
2. One of the safeties may be faulty: the limit switch (see page 23), or the fusible
link (see page 23).
3. The heating elements may not be working. See page 24 for directions on
testing and replacing them.
