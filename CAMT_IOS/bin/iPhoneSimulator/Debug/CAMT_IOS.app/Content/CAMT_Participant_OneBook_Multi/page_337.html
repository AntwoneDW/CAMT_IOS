Replacing a Filter
If the system is running, but is not as cool as it should be, the filter may need to be
replaced.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Wear eye protection.
  • Replacement filter
    • Wear sturdy gloves.
  • Lockout/tagout device
  • Screwdriver
    • How-to Steps
    1. Locate the filter(s) in the unit. Remove the housing as needed to access and remove the filter.
    2. Reinstall an exact replacement filter (be sure the size is the same), making sure that the area around the filter also is clear
    of debris. Take care to install the filter correctly. Many have arrows or indicators showing which direction the airflow should
    follow for correct filtration, performance, and lifespan.
    3. Replace the housing.
