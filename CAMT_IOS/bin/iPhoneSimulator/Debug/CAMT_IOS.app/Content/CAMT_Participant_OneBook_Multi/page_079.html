Multimeters
No maintenance tech should be without a multimeter—an inexpensive, versatile, battery-
operated tool you can use to measure electrical voltages, test for continuity in switches
and light fixtures, and test resistance in devices like heating elements.
More on the Multimeter
Display
Switch
Probes
The common multimeter is a small handheld device with a numeric digital display or an indicator needle over a measurement
scale. The device has a switch to select the type of test you want to perform. A multimeter also has two wires—one red and
one black—with metal tips. They are called probes.
