Identify Unmarked Compressor Terminals
Sometimes, the terminals on a compressor aren’t marked or are difficult to identify. Here’s
how to tell which terminal is which.
Introduction
Compressor motors have three terminals: a run (R) terminal, a start (S) terminal, and a common (C) terminal.
You can identify these terminals by using a multimeter to measure their resistance.
