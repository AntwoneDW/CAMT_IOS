When to Call a Heating Specialist
You’ll need to call a heating specialist when:
  • State or local regulations require it.
  • You don’t know how to do the repair—or feel uncomfortable doing it.
  • Your workload is too heavy, or you can’t do the repair quickly enough to satisfy a resident.
  • The job is physically too big for one person.
    • If a specialist comes on site to do a repair or replacement, watch what they’re doing and ask questions—you might be able to
    do some or all of the work if it happens again.
