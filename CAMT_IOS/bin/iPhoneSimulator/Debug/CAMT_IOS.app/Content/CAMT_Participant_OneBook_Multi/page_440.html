Replacing the Cooktop Igniter
Faulty igniters may sometimes keep burners from lighting.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Wear eye protection.
How-to Steps
1. Turn on other burner that uses the same igniter.
• If igniter sparks, first burner control is bad. Clean tip and retest.
2. Follow igniter cable to the terminal on the ignition control module. (This on the back of the cooktop.)
3. Remove the cover.
4. Disconnect the igniter cable.
5. Replace the igniter.
