How to Cut Plastic Pipe
1. Shut off the water to the pipe.
2. Position the pipe cutter (or hacksaw) on the pipe at a 90º angle (perpendicular)
3. Squeeze the handles together while rotating the blade around the pipe to begin cutting.
4. Once around the blade has cut into the pipe, continue to squeeze handles together.
5. Use a utility knife to clear off rough edges of pipe.
Note: Plastic pipe can also be cut with a hacksaw or circular saw. All cuts on plastic pipes should be at 90º.
How to Glue Plastic Pipe
Plastic pipe is joined with plastic fittings and solvent glue. Use a glue that is made for the type of plastic pipe you’re
