Make-ready Maintenance
Here are the key takeaways related to make-ready maintenance:
  • Learn how to see a just-vacated apartment through the eyes of the future resident, as well as those of your management
    • team or owner. Develop your “make-ready eye.”
  • Complete make-readies as quickly as you can without compromising quality. This can help your apartment community
    • minimize lost rent.
  • Use your company’s tracking tools to keep on top of make-ready inspections, work, and schedules.
  • Follow your company’s make-ready standards and procedures.
  • Suggest ways to improve the speed and quality of make-ready maintenance at your apartment community.
