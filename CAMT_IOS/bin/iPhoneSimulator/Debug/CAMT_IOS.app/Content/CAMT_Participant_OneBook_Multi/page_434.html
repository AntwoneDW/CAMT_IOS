Common Electric Cooktop Problems and Solutions
Electric cooktops often have problems with bad burner elements. It is important to
become skilled at recognizing cooktop problems to limit the amount of time you spend
testing and solving the problems.

Solution(s)

3. Defective burner switch. Check switch with multi-
meter and replace if necessary.
