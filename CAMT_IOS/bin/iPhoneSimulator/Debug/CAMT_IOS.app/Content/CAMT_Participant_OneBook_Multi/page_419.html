Problem
Solution(s)
Clothes are still soaking wet after the spin cycle
1. The water level switch may be faulty. Test and possibly
replace the water level switch; see page 33.
2. The motor and transmission may not be working
properly. You can test to see if the motor is faulty using
your multimeter, but repairs to the motor itself are best
left to a professional.
3. The pump may be blocked. Dissasemble the pump
from the hosing to determine if there is a blockage
inside the pump. See page 32 for instructions and a
video clip.
These are some common problems you may encounter in your role as a maintenance technician. It is not an all-inclusive list.
