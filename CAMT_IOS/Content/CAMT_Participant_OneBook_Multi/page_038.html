Tile: Replacing Tile
If tile is damaged, you can replace it without ruining the tiles around it.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
  • Wear eye and hand protection, as well as a mask.
  • Drill
  • Be careful using power tools, hammers, chisels, pry bars, and putty knives.
  • Hammer
  • Cold chisel
  • Pry bar
  • Putty knife
  • Notched trowel
  • Replacement tiles
  • Block of wood
  • Tile adhesive
  • Tile grout
  • Clean sponge
  • Clean cloth
  • Grout sealer
