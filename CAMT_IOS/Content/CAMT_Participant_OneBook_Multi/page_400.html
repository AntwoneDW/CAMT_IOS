Clothes Dryer Maintenance and Repair
How long would it take for a 12 oz. glass of water to fully evaporate if you set it on a
table? How long for a wadded up beach towel thrown into the hamper? Dryers serve one
purpose – they speed up evaporation time.
How a Clothes Dryer Works
A clothes dryer uses air, heat and motion to dry clothes, bedding, towels and other fabrics. They use a motor to turn a
drive belt, which spins the cylinder or drum with the wet laundry. A blower pushes heated air into the spinning cylinder to
convert liquid water into water vapor. This process is called evaporation. This mechanical system is the same for both gas
and electric dryers.
Dryer controls are used to adjust the drying time and temperature. Some dryers use mechanical controls and some controls
are electronic.
