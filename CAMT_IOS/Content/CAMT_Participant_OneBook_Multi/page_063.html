Comparing Electricity and Plumbing
You can immediately “see” how an electrical system works—at the highest of levels—by
comparing it to a plumbing system.
Electrical and Plumbing
The electrical system in an apartment works a lot like the plumbing system. Electricity flows through wires just as water flows
through pipes.
Electricity and water both enter the building, go to different areas to meet specific needs, and then move on.
Electricity is managed and controlled through receptacles, switches, and fixtures; water is managed and controlled through
faucets, showerheads, and toilets.
Electrical current leaves through non-pressurized (zero voltage) neutral wires. Water leaves through a non-pressurized
drain system.
