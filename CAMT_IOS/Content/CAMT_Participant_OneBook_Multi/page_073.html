Voltage Ratings
The voltage ratings determined by power companies and manufacturers have changed over the years. Current rated
at 110 volts changed to 115 volts, then 120 volts. Current rated at 220 volts changed to 230 volts, then 240 volts.
Similarly, ratings for receptacles, tools, light fixtures, and appliances have changed from 115 volts to 125 volts. These
changes don’t affect the performance of new devices connected to older wiring.
