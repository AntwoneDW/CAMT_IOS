Ceiling and Walls: Repairing Plaster
Plaster walls and ceilings can develop small to large cracks from drying or ground
movement. Here are some tips for repairing them.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
  • Wear eye protection and a mask.
  • Hammer
  • Be careful using a hammer and chisel.
  • Chisel
  • Putty knife
  • Soft bristle brush
  • Plaster patch material
  • A container to mix the plaster
  • Fine and very fine-grit
    • sandpapers
  • Primer
  • Paint
