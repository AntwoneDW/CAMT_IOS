How Toilets Work
Toilets have two main parts: tank and bowl. When the toilet handle is pushed, it pulls up a chain that lifts the flapper so water
flows from the tank into the bowl. As the water in the tank goes down, the float goes down with the water level. This closes
the flapper and triggers the fill valve to refill the tank with water.
