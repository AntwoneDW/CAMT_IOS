Replacing a Dishwasher Corner Seal
The corner seal is an easy part to replace. When water is running out of the door and
pooling on the floor – check the corner seal first.
Safety
Tools and Materials Needed
Wear eye protection when working with electrical systems.
  • Screwdriver
  • Corner seal
    • How-to Steps
    1. Remove the bottom dish rack.
    2. There is a corner seal in the bottom
    front corner on both sides. Use a
    screwdriver to remove the old corner
    seal on the side that is leaking.
    3. Replace with a new corner seal.
    4. Test to be sure the problem has
    been corrected.
