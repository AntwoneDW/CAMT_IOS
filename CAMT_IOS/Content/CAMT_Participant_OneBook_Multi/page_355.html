Check the Thermostat Operation
Faulty thermostats can also be the cause of air conditioning system malfunctions. Here
are two simple you can perform to make sure everything’s in order.
Introduction
When an air conditioner isn’t running, a relatively simple troubleshooting step is to check the thermostat.
Of course, you want to make sure that the thermostat is set to “cool” and an appropriate temperature, but you may also want
to check the input voltage of the thermostat, its fan circuit section, and its cooling circuit section.
