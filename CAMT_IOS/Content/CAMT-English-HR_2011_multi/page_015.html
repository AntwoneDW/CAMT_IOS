Neutral Wire
A wire that returns current at zero voltage to the source
of electrical power. Usually covered with white or light
gray insulation.
Ohms
Units of electrical resistance.
Overload
This happens when the demand for current is more than
the circuit wires or electrical devices were designed to
carry. Usually causes a fuse to blow or a circuit breaker
to trip.
Overloaded Circuit
A circuit that is carrying more power than what it was
designed to carry.
