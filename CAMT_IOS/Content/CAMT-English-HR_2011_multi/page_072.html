Refrigerator
Compressor
Specialized pump that pulls cool low-pressure gas vapor
refrigerant from the evaporator through a pipe called a
suction line and compresses it. This increases the pressure
and temperature of the refrigerant to a high pressure high
temperature vapor, and pushes it out through a pipe called
discharge line.
Condenser Coils
The coils located outside, on the back or under the
refrigerator, let the hot refrigerant gas dissipate its heat.
Defrost Heater
A heating element energized to melt ice
in the evaporator coil during the defrost
cycle.
