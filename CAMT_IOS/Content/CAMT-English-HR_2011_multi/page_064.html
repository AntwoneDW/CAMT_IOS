Solenoid Valve
An electromechanical valve for use with liquid or gas. The
valve is controlled by an electric current through a solenoid
coil. Solenoid valves may have two or more ports: in the
case of a two-port valve the flow is switched on or off;
in the case of a three-port valve, the outflow is switched
between the two outlet ports. Multiple solenoid valves can
be placed together on a manifold.
