Intake Valve
Water valve operated by an
electric solenoid that when
energized allow water into
the machine.

Pump
An electric motor powers the
pump. During the wash cycle,
the pump forces water up
into the spray arms. During
the drain cycle, the pump
directs the water into the
drain hose.
Pump
Spray Arm
Water is pumped up into the arm or arms through the
tower during the wash cycle. The arm rotates, spraying
water throughout the appliance.
