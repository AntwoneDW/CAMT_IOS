Motor
There are two types of machines: A direct-drive clothes
washer uses gears on the motor to turn the agitator and
spin the drum. A belt-drive washer transfers power from
the motor to the agitator and drum using a belt-and-pulley
system.
Pump
Circulates water
throughout the machine
during different cycles,
and drains dirty water out
during the drain cycle.
