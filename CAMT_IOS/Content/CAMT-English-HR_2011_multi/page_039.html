Refrigerants
The chemical that circulates
throughout the air conditioning
system, absorbing and expelling
heat as it is boiled and condensed.
The most common refrigerant
currently used in apartment
cooling systems is R-22. Newer
systems, however, use more
environmentally friendly refrigerant
such as R-410A to comply with
Refrigerants
government regulations.
Return Air Plenum
Warm air from inside the apartment enters through
the return air plenum, due to the blower pulling it from
the living space. This air is then moved across the
evaporator coil.
