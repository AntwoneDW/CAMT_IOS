Amp (or Ampere)
The rate at which electrical power flows to a light, tool,
or appliance. Large wires can carry more electricity than
small wires. The amount of current flowing through a wire
is called amperage.
Arc Flashes
A short circuit through the air. In an arc flash, a huge
amount of energy explodes outward from electrical
equipment.
