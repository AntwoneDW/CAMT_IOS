Water Heater Temperature & Pressure Relief
Valve (T & P Valve)
All water heaters have a safety device called a temperature
and pressure relief valve to let water escape if the water
pressure or temperature exceeds the limits of the tank.
Water Heater Thermostat
A device that senses when the temperature drops below
a certain pre-set temperature and causes the heating
element (in electric water heaters) or the gas burner
(in gas water heaters) to turn on.
Water Supply
Water is distributed throughout the city in large
underground pipes which makes its way to apartments,
businesses, and homes. Water meters track the amount
of water used at each location.
