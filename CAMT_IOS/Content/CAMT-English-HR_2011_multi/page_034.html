Evaporator Coil
Coils that allow refrigerant to absorb
heat from the indoor air, causing it to
increase in temperature, change into
a vapor, and then evaporate. This
evaporation process produces the
Evaporator Coil
cooling effect in the building.
Input Power Distribution Circuits
The source of power for the air conditioning unit, usually
consists of the power wiring from the main electrical
service head, to a disconnect switch near the unit.
It then travels back from this switch to the unit itself.
These circuits also include protective devices such as
fuses and circuit breakers.
